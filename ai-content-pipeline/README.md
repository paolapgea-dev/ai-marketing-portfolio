# AI Content Operations Platform

A custom AI-powered content operations system built to support Talaera's SEO, content production, publishing, and optimization workflows.

The platform combines AI research, content generation, SEO intelligence, performance monitoring, and automated content auditing into a single workflow. Rather than relying on isolated AI prompts, it provides a structured system for planning, creating, optimizing, publishing, and improving content over time.

The platform supports the full content lifecycle:

* Content research and keyword discovery
* SERP and competitor analysis
* Content briefing and drafting
* SEO optimization
* Internal linking recommendations
* AI-generated featured images
* WordPress publishing workflows
* Search performance monitoring
* Automated content audits and optimization recommendations

The platform is used internally by Marketing, Customer Success, Sales, and Leadership teams, creating a shared source of truth for content performance and strategic decision-making.

## Overview

Creating high-quality content at scale is difficult.

Most AI content workflows rely on a single prompt and produce generic outputs that require extensive editing. We wanted a system that could support a repeatable content process while preserving editorial quality, SEO standards, and strategic control.

Instead of relying on a single generation step, the platform breaks content creation into specialized stages including keyword research, SERP analysis, competitor intelligence, content brief generation, drafting, SEO validation, image creation, publishing, and ongoing optimization.

The goal is simple:

**Combine AI speed with human judgment, SEO best practices, and strong brand alignment.**

## Business Impact

The system helps scale content production while maintaining quality and consistency.

By combining proprietary business knowledge, brand guidelines, SEO best practices, and AI workflows, it reduces manual effort across the content lifecycle and enables faster content creation, optimization, and reporting.

The platform also creates a feedback loop between publishing and performance. Search data, content health signals, and engagement metrics continuously inform future content decisions, allowing the system to improve over time rather than operating as a one-way publishing pipeline.

## How It Works

The workflow breaks content creation into independent stages that can be reviewed, edited, and improved individually.

### Stage 1: Research & Keyword Discovery

* Generate aligned keyword opportunities
* Select target keywords from research
* Analyze search intent and SERP landscape

### Stage 2: Competitive Intelligence

* Identify competing content
* Extract competitor insights
* Aggregate strengths, weaknesses, and content gaps

### Stage 3: Content Planning

* Generate detailed content briefs
* Define structure, positioning, and key messages

### Stage 4: Draft Creation

* Generate article sections
* Enrich research
* Refine and consolidate content

### Stage 5: Optimization

* Place relevant CTAs
* Validate SEO requirements
* Generate AI-search-friendly query variations

### Stage 6: Production

* Generate featured images
* Prepare WordPress drafts
* Support publishing workflows

### Ongoing Content Monitoring

The platform doesn't stop once content is published.

Every article is continuously monitored through performance tracking and automated health checks that identify opportunities for optimization.

This creates a closed-loop system where publishing, performance, and future content decisions are connected.

## Health Check Engine

The platform automatically evaluates every article against ten weighted SEO and content-quality signals.

Rather than manually auditing hundreds of articles, the system identifies the pages most likely to benefit from intervention and prioritizes them by business impact.

Checks include:

* Position declines
* Click declines
* Low CTR relative to ranking position
* Indexation issues
* Internal linking gaps
* Missing external citations
* Content freshness
* Keyword-intent drift
* Thin content
* Poor ranking performance despite high impressions

Each issue receives a weighted severity score and is surfaced alongside AI-generated recommendations.

Examples:

* Refresh stale content
* Add internal links
* Fix canonicalization issues
* Improve SERP CTR
* Expand thin content
* Resolve keyword-intent mismatches

This allows content teams to focus effort where it will have the greatest impact rather than relying on manual audits.

## Performance Intelligence

Metrics include:

* Impressions
* Clicks
* Organic sessions
* Engagement
* Publishing velocity
* Funnel performance
* Pillar performance
* Content inventory health

Performance data informs future content creation decisions and feeds optimization workflows throughout the system.

## Shared Knowledge Layer

One of the most important aspects of the system is that it does not operate from prompts alone.

The pipeline uses centralized knowledge repositories that are injected into the generation process, including:

* Brand voice and tone guidelines
* Business context
* Content pillars
* Product and service information
* CTA libraries
* Proprietary platform statistics
* Visual design requirements

This allows content to remain aligned with company messaging while reducing prompt repetition and improving consistency across outputs.

This approach also allows updates to brand guidelines, positioning, products, or messaging to automatically propagate throughout future content generation workflows.

## Continuous Learning

The system is designed to evolve alongside content performance.

As new articles are published and performance data becomes available, workflows, prompts, and content strategies can be refined using real-world outcomes.

This creates a feedback loop between:

* Content production
* Search performance
* Audience engagement
* Editorial decision-making

Rather than generating content in isolation, the system supports ongoing optimization based on observed results.
